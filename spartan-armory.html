üéì About The Spartan Spirit Store

The Spartan Spirit Store is a student-led, school-based enterprise that has been serving the Bixby community since 2008.

This innovative program provides our students with invaluable hands-on experience in:

  • Customer Service - Direct interaction with community members
  • Retail Sales - Learning sales techniques and product knowledge
  • Visual Merchandising - Creating attractive product displays
  • Inventory Management - Tracking stock and managing supplies
  • Career Awareness - Exploring business and retail career paths

Our students gain real-world business experience while proudly serving the Bixby Spartan community. The Spartan Spirit Store operates as both an educational tool and a community service, helping students develop essential life skills while providing quality merchandise to our school and local community.

üíù Community Impact: $2 from every shirt sold goes directly to the American Cancer Society, teaching students about corporate social responsibility.
